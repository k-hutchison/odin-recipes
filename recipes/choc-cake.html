Chocolate Cake

Description

Elevation can cause a lot of changes with baking. At greater than 6000 feet above sea level, this chocolate cake is moist, holds it shape, and is packed full of chocolate flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 c flour
  • 1 3/4 c sugar
  • 3/4 c dutch processed cocoa
  • 2 tbs instant espresso powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 c whole milk, room temperature
  • 1 c sour cream, room temperature
  • 1 c vegetable oil
  • 1 tbs vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, and spray the bottoms of three 8 inch cake pans with non stick spray.
  2. In large bowel, sift flour, sugar, cocoa powder, espresso powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Separately, whisk together the eggs, milk, sour cream, oil and vanilla. Add the wet ingredients to the dry, and whisk until combined.
  4. Divide the batter between the pans. Bake on the center oven rack for about 22-28 minutes, until the tops of the cake spring back when lightly touched, or a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Bake until cookies are puffed and slightly brown, about 22 minutes.
  6. Set the pans on a cooling rack, cover loosely with a clean kitchen towel, and cool completely before frosting.
