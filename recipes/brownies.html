Brownies

Description

This is my favorite brownie recipe - it makes the perfect fudgy brownies with a crinkly top every time using melted chocolate chips rather than cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 2 TBS butter
  • 2 TBS water
  • 1 1/2 c chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2/3 c flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Grease 8 inch square pan.
  2. Combine sugar, butter and water in medium saucepan; cook and stir over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat and stir in chocoalte chips until melted and smooth; mix in eggs and vanilla. Combine flour, salt and baking soda; stir into chocolate mixture. Spread brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  4. Let the brownies cool completely before cutting into squares.
