Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This recipe is a copycat version of Levain Bakery's style of chocolate chip cookie. They are large and chock full of chocolate chips and nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c butter
  • 1/2 c brown sugar
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1 TBS vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of grated nutmeg
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 c flour
  • 2 1/2 c chocolate chips
  • 1 3/4 c pecans, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine butter, sugars, vanilla, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and nutmeg in bowel
  2. Cream at medium speed until soft, fluffy and pale; about 8 minutes. With mixer running, add eggs one at a time. Reduce speed to low and add flour all at once. When flour is incorporated, add chocolate chips and nuts and keep mixing until dough is homogenous
  3. Divide dough into 8 equal portions, and round equah itno a smooth ball. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for at least 12 hours.
  4. To bake, preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line aluminum pan with parchment paper. Arrange 4 portions of dough on pan.
  5. Bake until cookies are puffed and slightly brown, about 22 minutes.
  6. Cool cookies directly on baking sheet. Enjoy warm, or within 12 hours.
